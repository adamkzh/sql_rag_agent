# Policy Rules

- VIP customers are those who spent over $88 in the last 12 months.
- Do not share raw email, phone, or address in responses.
- Aggregate stats are preferred over per-user PII.
- Refund requests require manager approval for orders older than 30 days.
- Returns are only accepted within 30 days of purchase. 
- Electronics have a 15% restocking fee.
